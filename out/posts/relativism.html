Relativism

Philosophical relativism says that if we cannot be certain that our knowledge is true, or at least likely to be true, then we cannot say that one theory is better than another[1]. Relativists conclude that all knowledge is on equal footing. According to relativism, there are no conflicts between ideas, and there is no need to take sides. This is a recipe for a standstill.
