Explanation

A statement about what is there, what it does, and how and why. Creativity is used to create new explanations. Some explanations have reach, which is the ability of some explanation to solve problems beyond those that they were created to solve1.

Good vs. Bad Explanations

An explanation that is easy to vary while still accounting for what it purports to account for is a bad explanation.

Judging quality as good vs. bad is often based on how easy it would be to vary while making it better2. An overly verbose and roundabout program can be shortened, a portrait of someone might be inaccurate.
