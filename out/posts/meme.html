Meme

A meme is an idea that is a replicator. Memes can also form a memeplex that is a group of memes that help to cause each other's replication[1].

Types of Memes

  • Rational: an idea that relies on the recipients' critical faculties to cause itself to be replicated[1].
  • Anti-rational: an idea that replies on disabling the recipients' critical faculties to cause itself to be replicated[1].

Replication of Memes

Since there can be no replication of knowledge without interpretation (needs expansion), it also plays a vital component in the replication of memes, as creativity is required to faithfully replicate, e.g. a teacher's explanation of how multiplication works, inside of our own minds[2].
