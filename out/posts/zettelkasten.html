This workflow is from [1]:

  1. Take literature notes
    • Information you don't want to forget or think you'll use later in your own writing or thinking
    • They should be brief. Be extremely selective!
    • It has to be in your own words. No quoting.
    • (Use Kindle highlights to do these?)
  2. Take reference notes
    • Collection of literature notes
    • Summary of book
    • Who is the author?
  3. Make permanent notes
    • Most important step
    • Go over reference notes and look for connections between what you already know and link them together
    • Tie back into your interests
    • Create a page for the idea with a source, keywords (Important! "In which circumstances would I want to stumble upon this note?"), and relevant notes
    • Write notes in full proper sentences, no simple bullet-points.
  4. Review and repeat
